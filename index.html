About Me

My name is John LaTona and I am an up and coming web developer. I have a background in Business Management, having received my undergraduate degree from Monmouth University in 2009, and I recently graduated from Rutger's accelerated Web Development program. Since then I've been working in the automotive sales industry, with jobs in various departments in retail car dealerships. I took over a finance and insurance product agency in 2014 and was able to grow the business and merge with a larger company. I am now looking into diving headfirst into the tech industry as a developer.

I've had an interest in technology and software for a long time and I am very eager to continue to build functional, beautiful applications that make people's lives easier. Some of my hobbies include playing and writing music, traveling, reading, and spending time with my friends and family.
